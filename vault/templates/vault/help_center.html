Frequently Asked Questions
How does AI auto-summarization work?
When you capture a memory, MemoryVault sends the content to its AI engine, which generates a short snippet shown on the memory card. You can disable this anytime in Settings → AI Preferences.
Can I recover a deleted memory?
Yes. Deleted memories move to Trash and stay recoverable for 30 days by default (configurable in Settings → Privacy → Data Retention) before they're permanently purged.
How do Collections differ from Categories?
Categories are fixed memory types (Note, Voice Memo, Meeting, etc.), while Collections are custom folders you create to group memories around a project, topic, or goal.
Is my data shared to train the AI model?
Only if you opt in. The 'Allow AI model improvement' toggle in Settings → Privacy is off by default — your memories stay private unless you choose otherwise.
How do I export all of my data?
Go to Profile → Export Data or Settings → Security → Danger Zone, choose a format (JSON, CSV, or PDF report), and we'll email you a secure download link.
Can I use MemoryVault without an internet connection?
Capturing works offline and syncs once you're back online, but AI features like auto-summarization and the AI Assistant require a connection.
How do I change my notification preferences?
Head to Settings → Notifications to toggle the daily digest, weekly insights report, and anniversary reminders independently.
